Stream Companion

A Free Application to sort your Series, Movies and more!
Stream Companion is a free, fully responsive managing application for your series, movies and other things that you watch.
  • Developed in C# with .NET 4.0
  • Local and Online access
  • With an Integrated Stream Generator
  • Completely Free!
Requirements:
.Net 4.0 downloadable at microsoft.com

About

Stream Companion is 100% free to use. You can add as many Series, Movies or what ever you want to the list, set it as currently watching, completed, on hold, dropped or plan to watch.

Links to Streams will be automatically generated and updated once you increase your episode counter or reload them manually.

For future use, your list will be automatically uploaded for online accessibility.

Report a Bug

Thanks for your interest in Stream Companion! One of the best ways you can help us to improve is to let us know about any problems you find.

Here's how to report bugs:

  • Search for your bug to see if anyone has already reported it. Don't forget to search for all issues, not just open ones, as your issue might already have been reported and closed. To help find the most popular results, sort the result by votes.

  • If you find your issue and it's important to you, vote for it! That's how we know which bugs are most important to fix.

  • If no one's reported your bug, file the bug.

Keep in mind that an issue tracker is not a user support forum. It is a list of pending technical tasks, along with information relevant for those tasks, and information about progress on those tasks including which ones might get worked on in the short term.

In general, please put as much info in bugs as you can. Just a one liner telling us something isn't working is usually useless, and will probably be closed without any action. The more detail you provide, the more likely your issue is to be resolved.
